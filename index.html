A B O U T
koji (ko-jee): cultivate, heal, peace.

Koji is a boy’s name of Japanese origin, meaning “cultivate,” “heal,” and “peace.” Koji is a beautiful name that is simple to spell, pronounce, and easily trips off the tongue.

麹（こうじ）とは、「育​​てる」「癒す」「平和」を意味する日本語由来の男の子の名前です。麹は、つづりも発音も簡単で、舌をつまずきやすい美しい名前です。

Ideally located in the heart of Tokyo, the koji cocktail bar will provide you with the best cocktails one could ever dream of.
